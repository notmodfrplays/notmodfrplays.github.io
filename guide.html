Guide To Overall DDR improvements

Finding a location

If you are like me, and live in the San Francisco Bay Area, there are multiple places to play DDR that have e-amusement support. Some good examples could be Round1 which is a Japanese arcade chain or Dave and Busters. Smaller arcades or GolfLand also have DDR, and other music games.

What the hell is e-amusement?

e-amusement is an online service which allows you to keep scores and stats on the internet. All you need is a small initial payment for the card and that is it. (Round1 carry e-amusement cards, this is where I bought mine)
